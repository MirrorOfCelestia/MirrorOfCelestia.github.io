Introduction

Hi everyone! You can call me Akira (also known as MirrorOfCelestia). I know most of you might skip this part, so don’t worry — there’s no vital info here. That said, I just want to thank you for taking the time to check out this guide (and hopefully enjoy the game too)!

Pokémon Academy Life Forever has been one of my favorite dating sims — fun, charming, and full of heart. But I’ll be honest: it has a steep learning curve. It took me multiple playthroughs to really understand how things worked, and I often found myself searching online for help… only to come up empty.

That’s where this guide comes in. Whether you’re new or returning, this is meant to teach you everything you need to know to get the most enjoyable experience possible — before and during your playthroughs.

I’ve done my best to make this guide clear, helpful, and easy to follow, so you can focus on what matters: enjoying your time at the Academy.

If you have any questions, feedback (hopefully positive!), or constructive criticism, feel free to reach out. I’d love to hear from you and make this guide even better.

Good luck, and have fun!

— Akira Kagami (MirrorOfCelestia)

About Pokémon Academy Life Forever (PALF)

Pokémon Academy Life Forever is a fan-made visual novel and dating sim set in the Pokémon universe. It blends storytelling, character interaction, and RPG-style gameplay, offering a unique experience for Pokémon fans.

Story

Step into the shoes of a student at the prestigious Pokémon Academy, where friendships are formed, rivalries ignite, and your choices shape your destiny. Explore the school, interact with fellow students, and uncover mysteries along the way.
