About Pokémon Academy Life Forever (PALF)

Pokémon Academy Life Forever (PALF) is a non-profit, fan-made Ren’Py visual novel developed by Freudian Creations. It takes place in an alternate Pokémon world where characters from the core Pokémon series are placed in a university setting to learn about the world of Pokémon.

PALF is a remake of the original Pokémon Academy Life, a project that led to developer burnout and was eventually scrapped. The team at Freudian Creations took up the project and rebuilt it into what it is today.

Story

To become a Pokémon Champion has been your dream for as long as you can remember. But to achieve that dream, you first need to learn the ropes — and what better place than the prestigious Kobukan Academy?

After receiving an invitation to the academy, you’ll spend your time exploring the Kobukan region, catching and training Pokémon, and learning all about the world around you. Along the way, you might even form close bonds with new faces and old friends.

But Kobukan Academy is full of mysteries... including one pressing question: how will you ever pay off your university debt?
